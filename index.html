Information

As Columbus becomes a larger metropolitan city, there still seems to be a disparity in the quality of public school education. Franklin County has 16 school districts, we look to see if location, socio-economic issues, and teacher's qualifications affect a school districts performance. Our visualization will explore what factors impact a students performance the most.

Factors

Success %: Percentage of students who have one component of the success program outlined here

ACT %: Percentage of students who were ACT remedial free

Median Income: Median income percentage taken against the area's maximum

Diversity %: Percentage of students who come from minority groups

College Parent: Percentage of students who have one parent with a college degree

Teacher Experience: Percentage of teacher salary compared to the area's maximum

Teacher Salary: Percentage of teacher salary compared to the area's maximum

Teacher Desnity: Number of teachers per 1,000 students percentage taken against the area's maximum

Absent %: Percentage of students who fell under the chronic absenteeism threshold (More info)
